ACL 2025 extends its deepest gratitude to all its sponsors. The conference could not take place without the invaluable support provided by these generous organizations. We sincerely thank them for their continued commitment to the NLP/CL community. 


If your organization would like to sponsor the ACL, please refer to our [Sponsorship Booklet](/assets/Sponsorship%20brochure%20for%20ACL%202025%20conferences%20-%202025-01-03.pdf) for information about sponsorship rates and benefits. For any questions regarding sponsorship, please email the ACL Sponsorship Director Chris Callison-Burch (<sponsorship@aclweb.org>). 

The deadline to sponsor **ACL 2025 is Tuesday, June 10, 2025.**
